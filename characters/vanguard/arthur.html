CHARACTER INFORMATION




Arthur Kingsley

Lieutenant Arthur Kingsley is one of the four playable protagonists and Operator featured in Call of Duty: Vanguard. Kingsley was also released in Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War as an Operator as a Vanguard pre-order bonus on September 27th, 2021. Both his Black Ops Cold War and Vanguard versions are available in Call of Duty: Warzone. Born a royal in Cameroon, Arthur Kingsley studied at Cambridge and joined the British army to fight for the free world. A true leader, Arthur protects those whose serve beside him, earning their undying loyalty and respect

Born into a noble family of the Duala people in British Cameroon, Kingsley attended the University of Cambridge in England at the age of 16 at the behest of his father. After studying international affairs, learning many languages along the way, Kingsley graduated in 1936 and joined the British Army.

In 1939, Kingsley was deployed in France as part of the British Expeditionary Force after the British Empire declared war on Nazi Germany following the invasion of Poland. The next year, he was one of the soldiers from the Battle of Dunkirk who made it back to England.

In June 1944, now a Paratrooper Sergeant, Kingsley participated in Operation Tonga as part of the Normandy landings. Following his brave actions during the operation, he was assigned as the leader of the Special Operations Task Force 001, also known as Task Force Vanguard, and was deployed in a submarine base in Germany in search of intel regarding the plans of the Nazi High Command following the fall of Berlin.

In April 1945, now promoted to Lieutenant, Kingsley was placed in charge of a special forces unit operating under the British Special Operations Executive, tasked with obtaining information on Project Phoenix.



TRIVIA


  • Arthur Kingsley is based on Sidney Cornell.
  • Kingsley's blood-type is O+.
  • Kingsley's birthday is claimed to be February 16, 1916 on Black Ops Cold War, however his birthday
    in Vanguard states he was born on September 27, 1915.
  • In Black Ops Cold War, Kingsley's quotes when performing Finishing Moves is more varied compared
    to other operators.
