WELCOME TO WAR, SOLDIER!

Call of Duty is the world's best-selling combat video game franchise. Beginning with its first entry in 2003, most Call of Duty games are first-person shooters that allow players to immerse themselves as soldiers in wartime scenarios. Call of Duty , one of the most long-standing first-person shooter games in history, is aiming to reinvent itself by returning to the moniker that changed the landscape of multiplayer games.

This wiki documents some of the games in the franchise, including:



  • VANGUARD
  • BLACK OPS COLD WAR
  • WARZONE
